ABOUT CLIENT

Hostopia is a leading provider of private label web services and business communication tools that allow service providers to differentiate their core product offerings, drive incremental revenue and build customer retention.

PROBLEM

A restaurant, retail store and construction business do not share all the same business needs. A verticalized approach was needed to help advise these businesses on the tools they needed to succeed online.

Research

With many service providers as our partners, we can get access to a wide variety of data. We researched which industries most of our user's small business fell under. Our findings pointed to restaurant, retail, and consultant as the top three industries.


With this knowledge we determined which of our partners core products, might be most useful to their customers small business and gathered a list of recommendation for each. A restaurant could benefit from being listed in multiple online directories while a consultant may be working from home and prefer not to list their address but could benefit more from SEO.

Challenges

  1. Building trust
  2. Focused demographic
  3. Collecting user data

Solution

The online product advisor acts as a lead generation and sales enablement tool. By collecting and capturing information from prospective user a personalized recommendation is made. Displayed on an engaging landing page these recommendations for solutions and products are verticalized towards the user's industry and tailored to their business needs.

Learnings

Choosing what we won't do

There were many great use cases we could tackle with a rich feature set. However, creating a vertical for single one was costly or unrealistic. I had to determine where the real value was for us so we did not spread ourselves too thin and could provide a proof of concept before adding more.
